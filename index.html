Batman's Characteristics

  1. Batman is the alias of Bruce Wayne, a wealthy American playboy, philanthropist, and industrialist who resides in Gotham City.
  2. He has trained his body and mind to near physical perfection to be a self-made Hero.
  3. He's developed an arsenal of technology that would put most armies to shame.
